Legal Rights for Women and LGBTQ+ in India

  • Right to report harassment without parental permission (for minors).
  • Zero FIR: Can be filed at any police station, regardless of jurisdiction.
  • Right to privacy while reporting sexual assault.
  • Protection against workplace sexual harassment (POSH Act).
  • Section 377 decriminalization - LGBTQ+ rights recognized.
  • Free legal aid is available for women and LGBTQ+ individuals.
